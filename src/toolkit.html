Production Engineer Toolkit

Overview

Role

UX Designer

Timeframe

Ongoing

Team

  • UX Designer
  • 3 Developers
  • Business Analyst
  • Product Owner

Client

A leading oil and gas company aiming to streamline the workflow of their production engineers.

Objective

Develop a comprehensive production engineer toolkit to provide production engineers with a centralized platform for viewing insights, managing tasks, and making informed decisions. This toolkit integrates data from various reports, enabling engineers to access all necessary information and track assigned tasks efficiently.

Process

The project involves ongoing collaborative meetings with the BA and PO to establish and refine design requirements. Based on these discussions, I create initial design concepts and iterate upon feedback. Presenting my designs to the development team ensures alignment and allows for the clarification of any technical questions. I also create coded prototypes to demonstrate design behavior as the project has transitioned to development. Balancing developer bandwidth with expectations has been crucial to ensure that the final designs are feasible and impactful. The current status involves continuing to refine the design, awaiting the first release, and planning further refinements based on user feedback.
