Team

Two designers, a UX researcher, data scientist, BA, and a team of subject matter experts (SMEs) from the client side.

Client

A leading energy retail company striving to enhance operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Objective

Conceptual designs for a comprehensive product suite that includes a CRM, an executive dashboard, and a consumer portal.

Strategy

This project was both large and complex, constrained by a very short timeframe. Our initial step was to rapidly design and conduct workshops to capture the general requirements for the conceptual designs. Given the industry's diverse regulations across different regions, numerous edge cases were anticipated, leading us to focus on illustrating the core value through a "happy path" approach, rather than attempting to develop a fully detailed product.

Following this strategic focus, we divided the design workload among team members. This ensured that all essential tasks were efficiently completed before the deadline, adhering to the project's ambitious schedule.
