Energy Retail Product Suite

Overview

Role

UX Designer

Timeframe

8 weeks

Team

  • 2 UX Designers
  • Behavioral Scientist
  • Data Scientist
  • Business Analyst
  • Client Subject Matter Experts

Client

A leading energy retail company aiming to increase internal productivity and improve employee and customer satisfaction.

Objective

Develop conceptual designs for a comprehensive product suite to showcase potential offerings and guide future development. This suite included:

  • CRM
  • Data Dashboards
  • Consumer Portal

Strategy

Given the project's large scope and short timeframe, we conducted workshops to gather general requirements for the conceptual designs. Due to the industry's diverse regulations across regions, we focused on illustrating the core value through a "happy path" approach, prioritizing essential features over complete detail.

To manage the workload efficiently, design tasks were divided between me and the other UX designer, ensuring all design deliverables were completed within the project's short schedule.

Personal Responsibilities

  • Collaborated with the client in workshops to understand consumer portal flows.
  • Designed three separate dashboards for specific internal users, each with different data concerns.
  • Created a CRM flow, including designing complex tables and processes for sending out and renewing energy plans.
